LeBron James sets an NBA record playing his 1612th game

LeBron James set a new NBA record for most games played, appearing in his 1,612th career game to pass Robert Parish. The milestone adds to James’ long list of career achievements, as he continues to perform at a high level deep into his 21st season.

Victor Wembanyama takes 1st place on the NBA MVP Ladder

Victor Wembanyama just flipped the MVP race on its head, jumping past Shai Gilgeous-Alexander and Luka Dončić to take the top spot on the ladder. Luka Doncic has been on an absolute heater, averaging 40 points across March and carrying his team night after night. Meanwhile, Shai’s consistency all season still keeps him right there in the race, but right now, it’s Wemby’s moment at the top.

NBA fines Portland Trailblazers $100,000

The National Basketball Association fines the Portland Trailblazers $100,000 for their illegal interactions with Yang Hansen. Portland's personnel team made these interactions in December 2023, 2 years before Yang was drafted. NBA teams are prohibited from talking to draft prospects before they declare for the NBA Draft to maintain a fair drafting system.
